<span style="color:lightsteelblue;font-size:72px;font-weight:bold">parboiled</span>

_parboiled_ is a mixed Java/Scala library providing for lightweight and easy-to-use, yet powerful and elegant parsing
of arbitrary input text based on "Parsing expression grammars":http://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Parsing_expression_grammar (PEGs).
PEGs are an alternative to "context free grammars":http://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Context-free_grammar (CFGs) for formally
specifying syntax, they make a good replacement for regular expressions and generally have quite a few advantages over
the "traditional" way of building parsers via CFGs.
_parboiled_ is released under the "Apache License 2.0":http://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Apache_license.
